---
title: DateTime
---


###	Year
> datetime.year

###	Month number
> datetime.month

###	Day of the month
> datetime.day

###	Hours of the day, 24h format
> datetime.hours

###	Minutes of the current hour
> datetime.minutes

###	Seconds of the current minute
> datetime.seconds

###	Custom time format
> datetime.format

See [PHP date documentation](https://www.php.net/manual/en/function.date.php) for available formats
